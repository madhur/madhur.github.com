DashAnalytics

DashAnalytics for DashClock

DashAnalytics is an extension for DashClock Widget

DashAnalytics displays the metrics from your Google Analytics Website or App profile. Very useful to track on regular basis metrics such as Daily Visitors, visits, new visits for your Website or Mobile application.

It is available for free in Google Play Store

WebSite Metrics Displayed

  • Visitors
  • Visits
  • Percentage new visits
  • New visits

Mobile App Metrics Displayed

  • Visitors
  • Visits
  • Percentage new visits
  • New visits
